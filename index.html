About me

PROFESSIONAL PATH

“The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don’t know” - Albert Einstein

Ever since I first heard this quote, I have found it inspiring. For me, the quote does not depict a daunting abundance of ungraspable knowledge. Rather, it speaks to the endless adventures in understanding that are possible. I love to learn and to develop new skills. I especially like to challenge myself on topics I find particularly difficult. I also like to use newly acquired knowledge to produce results that are far beyond what is expected of me.

The seemingly endless learning opportunities is one of the reasons that software development is a passion of mine. I am fascinated by every facet of it. This includes everything from UX to devops and from writing documentation to styling best practices. My eagerness to learn is constantly being satisfied by the amount of knowledge that already exists and the cascade of new developments that occur in the industry. During my studies at Codeworks, I have had the opportunity to work on a team to develop web applications. This experience ignited a desire in me to experience working on teams of varying sizes and for learning project management paradigms such as Agile and SCRUM. I want to work with others who possess the same passion for learning that I do. I want to work in an environment that encourages learning and the sharing of knowledge between peers. I believe that this attitude and environment leads to high productivity and to a healthy and motivated work force.
